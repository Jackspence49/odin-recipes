Smashed Potatoes

Description:

An easy recipe for crispy yet soft and fluffy smashed Potatoes!

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Slat
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Cut the Potatoes in half and bl for 30 minutes
  2. Pre heat oven to 390
  3. Dump potatoes out of the boiling water and let them dry for 5 minutes
  4. Smash them using a cup
  5. Drizzle with olive oil
  6. Bake them for 55 minutes
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste
