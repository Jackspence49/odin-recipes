Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description:

The best chocolate chip cookies recipe ever! No funny ingredients, no chilling time. Just simple, amazing chocolate chip cookies that turn out perfectly every single time

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • butter
  • Chocolate
  • Vanilla extract
  • salt
  • baking powder
  • eggs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375. Line three baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside
  2. In a bowl mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Set aside
  3. Cream together butter and sugars until combined
  4. beat in eggs and vanilla until light
  5. mix in the dry ingredients until combined
  6. add chocolate chips and mix well
  7. Roll dough into balls and place them evenly spaced on your cookie sheets
  8. Bake in preheated oven for approximately 8-10 minutes.
