Light and Fluffy Scrambled Eggs

Description:

This recipe is for creating light and salty scrambled eggs. This

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Combine the eggs, salt, and milk in a medium bowl and whisk until homogeneous and frothy, about 1 minute. Allow to rest at room temperature for 15 mins. The eggs should darken in color
  2. Melt the butter in the skillet over medium high heat
  3. Add eggs into the pan and continue to mix until the eggs form a solid
  4. Once the eggs form a solid stop stiring and take out of the pan
